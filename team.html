Our Story

We're a team of first-generation college students driven to make room-sharing feel like home. We believe the right roommate can turn shared spaces into meaningful connections, and we're dedicated to helping you find that perfect match. With LivSync, we’re here to make shared living simpler, friendlier, and more like family.
